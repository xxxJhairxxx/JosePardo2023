Education & Career Fair Is A One Stop Education Exhibition Which Is Focused On Student Recruitment. It Is One Of The Most Reputed Education Fair In World Organized By Education

Exhibitions | Education & Career Fair | Expo

Education & Career Fair is a one stop education exhibition focused on student recruitment after the Board examination result. This event takes place every year when students are searching various career options for admissions in the institutes of their choice. It is a cost effective marketing platform for the Universities, Colleges, & Educational Institution to reach out a large number parents & students who are interested for further education. It is an excellent platform for Class 12th Pass outs & College students to target premier recognized Universities/Colleges in India for pursuing UG & PG programs all under one roof. This event is of two days with fabricated stalls at the center of the location. Last year Education did this event at Bhilwara in association with Dainik Bhaskar, one of the leading newspaper.

Why to Participate

Interactive Platform: An excellent platform to interact face-to-face with thousands of career aspirants. Communicate directly to the visitors about your institutions, its unique features, admission procedure and costs.

Spot Enrolments & Admission: At the Seminar, conduct on the spot students enrolments & offer admission to later contact. the deserving candidates. Generate inquiries and leads for later contact

Highly Cost Effective Medium: Highly cost effective and strong marketing platform to connect with a large section of target audience. With minimum expense maximum returns can be derived to enrol the right profile of candidate from the driven seminar.

Brand Building Medium: Enhance the brand of your institutions by showcasing the strengths & uniqueness of your institutions among the affluent & eager students community.

Media Coverage: Campaign will be driven through extensive media coverage through series of print ads, edit coverage & other medium

Promotion in Print: Publish a teaser ad for the announcement of fair 10 days in advance. A full page ad on the inaugural day of fair followed by half page during the event as per requirement. All these ads will carry logo of all the sponsors and participants.

Talk Show: 10 days prior to fair, we will organise a talk show with our top clients and due editorial coverage will be published next day.

News Coverage: An editorial about the event shall be published well before the event promoting the education category as well. During the event good editorial coverage will be given in our newspaper highlighting the response to the event, spot admissions, and offers for the students and new courses.

Activities to increase footfall: We will do a seminar and "Career Counselling Sessions" with leading educationalist from Rajasthan and motivational speakers.
